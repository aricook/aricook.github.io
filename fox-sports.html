competitive & comparative analysis

I conducted a competitive analysis to find out what types of functionality suited users best when comparing products. From this I drew to the conclusion that when comparing products people want a clear, simple layout. The challenge was then to find out what would help engage users without being too prominent and distracting.

After conducting a heuristic evaluation on the current site, My solution was to utilise the user query to link the type of sport they were looking at previously into the header image and make that particular sport more prominent within the comparison chart.

the solution.

A fully responsive website from desktop through to mobile with a more functional and engaging experience than the previous version.

the outcome.

Iterative design & prototype development.

I started by sketching the wires which I tested and iterated many times.

I then produced a more high fidelity interactive mobile mockup using Illustrator, Css and HTML5 for a presentation with the stakeholders to show key interactions within the app.

Having supplied the client with 2 UX options, one with major and one with minor changes. The client purchased both concepts to roll out at different stages. After the 2 concepts were approved I then brought the wireframes to life with a motivating, on-brand interface design.
